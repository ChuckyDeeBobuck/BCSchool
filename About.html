Wanna Know A Little About Me?

Hi, my name is Ray Wade. I spent a lot of my life in Anaheim California. I worked at Disneyland for 17 years. During that time, I went to The Art Institute, and received a BS in Game Art and Design. After graduation, I left Disneyland, and started working in the Video Game Industry. There I made games for Playstion, Xbox,and Nintendo. Ironically, I landed here in Washington as a video game design for Disney Interactive. Later, I began to make my own games. I released a game called Pixel Sand on Steam. Because of that game, I became interested in programming.

Because Washington is so beautiful, I decided to stay here. I went back to school, and received a Certificate in C#/C++ from Lake Washington Institute. I decided to continue my education at Bellevue College where I am studying programming. Currently I am taking 3 classes:
